WHO WE ARE

Hello Co-Food Lovers!!! Just like you even we were tired of trying different restaurants around, browsing through various food delivery apps or home deliveries and sometimes cooking after pulling out time from a very busy schedule. Though these were good options, they could only serve as a change from routine but were never a healthy solution. After a sour chase for the healthy routine, frustrated we, gave up finding for one. We need home like food in a home like routine. Any given day, however busy we are, our favorite food should be waiting on the table. We friends being from different fields(a doctor, few techies from IIT, an IIM alumni, an Australia returned Chef) teamed up and after lot of brainstorming sessions, discussions, market analysis and research, came up with this concept of Meal Time. We took this seriously and joined few more passionate guys to finish the recipe, well garnished and glazing too. Now we are successfully operating in most of the areas in Hyderabad. Lets put an end to the coordination efforts we face for our daily food.
